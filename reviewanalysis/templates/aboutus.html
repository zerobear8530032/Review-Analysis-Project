About Us

We're just a group of college students passionate about product reviews!

Our Mission

At ReviewAnalysis, our mission is simple: to help fellow students and consumers make informed decisions about products. We believe that understanding reviews can save time and money, and we want to make that process as easy as possible!

Our Journey

ReviewAnalysis started as a class project in our Data Science course at JNCT College. What began as a simple idea quickly turned into a full-fledged project as we realized the need for better tools to analyze product reviews.
